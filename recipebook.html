Omelette rice

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
onion 1/2
chicken meat 1
mixed vegitables 1/2 cup
rice 2 servings
ketchup 1 Tbsp
large egg 2

Step 1: Cut onions and chicken.

Step 2: Heat onions, chicken and other vegetables.

Step 3: Add rice and ketchup and fry.

Step 4: Bake the stirred eggs.

Step 5: Wrap chicken rice in eggs.

Source: Omurice (Japanese Omelette Rice) オムライス

Fried Shrimp

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
prawn 1lb
all-purpose flour 1/2 cup
large eggs 3
panko 2 cups
neutral-flavored oil 4 cups
salt and pepper a little

Step 1: Season the prawn with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Dredge the prawns with flour, eggs and panko.

Step 3: Put the shrimp in oil.

Step 4: Remove the shrimp from the oil.

Step 5: Please enjoy with tartar sauce.

Source: Ebi Fry (Japanese Fried Shrimp) エビフライ

Mill crêpes

  • Time: 40 mins
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
whole milk 1.75 cup
suger 3 Tbsp
large eggs 3
butter 1.5 Tbsp
cake flour 1 cup
heavy cream 1 cup

Step 1: Add the milk and sugar in a small sauce pan and combine well.

Step 2: Beat the eggs in a large bowl. Then slowly add a small amount of warm milk while whisking.

Step 3: Sift the rest of dry ingredients and mix until there is no lump in the butter.

Step 4: Set a 12-inch non-stick frying pan on low heat.

Step 5: Layer the crepe and whipped cream.

Source: Matcha Mille Crepe Cake (Video) 抹茶ミルクレープケーキ
