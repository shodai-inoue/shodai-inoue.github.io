Carry my bag and my bottle

Posted on October 16 in 2021

Sustainable Development Goal 14

I carry my bags and bottles with me to reduce plastic waste. When I'm shopping at convenience stores and supermarkets, I use my bag instead of getting a plastic bag. When I go out, I bring a my bottle and try not to buy PET bottles as much as possible. Also, when I buy a PET bottle, I try to separate it properly.

Reduce food loss

Posted on October 17 in 2021

Sustainable Development Goal 12

To reduce food loss, I try to get rid of leftover food and avoid shopping too much. In order to reduce the amount of food waste, I try to eat foods with the closest expiration date. When shopping at supermarkets and convenience stores, I try not to buy inedible amounts of ingredients.

Save electricity and water

Posted on October 18 in 2021

Sustainable Development Goal 7

By actively saving electricity and water, I am able to make effective use of limited resources. I try to turn off the lights in unused rooms and not waste too much water. Although it is a slight difference, saving electricity and water will help reduce living expenses.
